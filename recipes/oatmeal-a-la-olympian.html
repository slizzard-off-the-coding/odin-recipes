Oatmeal à la Olympian

Description

Rise and dine . . . like an Olympian.

Enjoy this oatmeal for breakfast, and be ready to defeat the day!

Ingredients

  • Oats
  • Milk
  • Blueberries
  • Chia seeds

Steps

  1. Pour 1 cup* milk into pot/pan over medium-high heat
  2. Once milk is hot, stir in 1 1/2 cups* oats
  3. Cook for 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally
  4. Remove from heat and let stand for 2 minutes
  5. Serve oatmeal and top with blueberries and chia seeds - mix well and enjoy!

* For thinner oatmeal, use more milk or less oats. For thicker oatmeal, use less milk and more oats.
